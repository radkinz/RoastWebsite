About The Event

Steer Roast is a weekend gathering of Senior House residents, friends, and alumni which has been held annually since 1963. Events include the ceremonial lighting of the barbeque pit, two nights of live music, the festive meal, the awards ceremony, and more.

Since the shutdown of Senior House, Steer Roast has been held at various venues in and around Cambridge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the date different this year?

The reason we moved Roast up is to ensure that we still align with ESF.

The first weekend of May overlaps with Springfest, a bit MIT wide event. When East Campus has it's courtyard this overlap does not pose a problem, but due to EC renovations, this provides a significant issue for ESF. Last year, ESF was unable to have any large outdoor space on Saturday because of Springfest, which is a major deal since ESF traditionally is an event that primarily only happens on Saturday. It was also a great headache for the ESF chairs and CAC to deal with the overlap in events since CAC can only accommodate so many of their employees to dealing with the event's setup, clean up, on-call managers, etc. .

Where will Roast be held?

The main venue will be the Cambridge Masonic Lodge which is located at 1950 Massachusetts Avenue
